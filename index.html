Montana News


Montana PBS Reports is the News/Public Affairs series covering topics important to Montanans. A team of correspondents travel to Montana cities and small towns to provide in-depth reporting and investigative research focusing on the people affected by these issues.

Meet Your News Team

Breanna McCabe

Producer


Breanna McCabe started reporting in her home state of Montana in 2009. After her journalism career took her to CBS affiliates in Missoula and Spokane, she landed at Montana PBS in 2020, where she also regularly contributes to "Backroads of Montana.”

Stan Parker

Producer


Stan Parker is a multimedia journalist who focuses on Eastern and Central Montana. He joined MontanaPBS in 2022, bringing with him a decade of experience in newsrooms and independent documentaries. He lives in Billings with his wife, Tori.

Anna Rau

Producer


Anna Rau is an Investigative Journalist with nearly 30 years of experience in news and documentary production. Her investigative work has been honored with a National Cine Golden Eagle Award, a Telly Award, and an EB Craney award, and she was recently named a finalist for a National Investigative Reporters and Editors Award.

AJ Williams

Producer


Aj Williams is a documentary producer and contributor for Montana PBS Presents: IMPACT. Williams joined KUFM staff in 2021, and is a graduate of the University of Montana’s Environmental Science & Natural Resource Journalism graduate program.

Hannah Kearse

Producer


Hannah Kearse’s interests in the outdoors and storytelling began in the Lowcountry of South Carolina. She followed her desires for higher ground and education to the University of Montana. Starting her career as a newspaper reporter in Madison County, Hannah continues to expand her love for Montana journalism in broadcast.

Sam Wilson

Producer


Before coming to Montana PBS, Sam Wilson worked in print media for a decade as a photojournalist, the last three at the Bozeman Daily Chronicle. He is a graduate of the University of Montana

SUPPORTED BY
